Privacy Policy

NU Results Portal

Last updated: April 16, 2026

NU Results Portal provides access to National University result pages, notices, PDF resources, and related information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information the app handles

  • Result details shown on official National University pages, which may include student name, roll number, registration number, CGPA, and course results.
  • Search history saved locally on your device so recent result lookups can be shown again inside the app.
  • Theme preference saved locally on your device.
  • Device, advertising, and diagnostic signals that may be collected by Google Mobile Ads or other third-party services used by the app.
  • PDF files and related resources that you choose to open, cache, or download through the app.

How information is used

  • To display official result, notice, and document content.
  • To remember recent result lookups on your device.
  • To remember app settings such as theme selection.
  • To display ads and support operation of the app.
  • To troubleshoot app reliability and performance issues.

Local storage

The app stores certain information locally on your device, including recent search history and theme preference. PDF files may also be cached or saved to device storage when you open or download them.

Web content and official NU websites

The app loads content from official National University websites and result pages inside in-app web views or through direct requests. Information you enter into official result forms is submitted to the relevant National University website, which may process that data under its own privacy practices.

Advertising and third-party services

The app uses third-party services, including:

  • Google Mobile Ads
  • Official National University websites and web resources

These third parties may collect or process information according to their own privacy policies. You can review Google's policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Sharing

If you use the app's share feature, the information or image you choose to share is sent only through the app or service you select.

Children

The app is not designed specifically for children under 13. If you are submitting the app for an audience that includes children, review your Play Console declarations carefully and ensure they match your actual audience and data practices.
