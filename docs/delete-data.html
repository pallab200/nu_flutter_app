How to request deletion

To request deletion of your personal data associated with this app, please email the developer at tapallab00@gmail.com with the subject line:

Delete my data — NU Results Portal

In your message, include the device ID (if available), approximate device model, and a short description of the data you want deleted. We will confirm receipt and respond within 14 days.

What data will be deleted

The developer will delete the following data types upon verified request:

  • Local search history stored by the app (saved in device SharedPreferences).
  • Locally cached PDF files and temporary files stored in the app's documents/cache directory.

What data is not deleted

The following items cannot be deleted by the developer because they are not stored by the developer or are controlled by third parties:

  • Advertising identifiers and ad-related signals collected by Google Mobile Ads — managed by Google and subject to Google's policies. To opt out of personalized ads, follow device-level ad settings or Google's ad settings documentation.
  • Content hosted on third-party websites that the app links to or opens in a webview (for example, university notices on external sites) — you must contact those site owners.

Retention period

Unless you request deletion earlier, the app retains local search history and cached files on the device until they are removed by the user or the app is uninstalled. After a verified deletion request, retained data described above will be removed within 30 days.

Verification and follow-up

To protect user privacy, we may request additional information to verify your identity and ownership of the data before processing deletion requests. If we cannot verify your request, we'll explain what is needed.
